The World's Premier Engineering Innovation Ecosystem

Since 1962, CIET has been the catalyst for groundbreaking engineering solutions that shape our world. Our consortium connects visionary engineers with cutting-edge resources, funding opportunities, and collaborative partnerships to accelerate technological advancement.

We specialize in cutting-edge projects across aerospace, biomedical engineering, sustainable energy systems, artificial intelligence, robotics, and advanced materials. Our portfolio includes everything from breakthrough medical devices to next-generation aerospace components.

Our partnership process begins with an initial consultation to understand your goals. We then match you with relevant experts, resources, and funding opportunities. Throughout the project, you'll have access to our global network, technical facilities, and mentorship programs.

Members gain exclusive access to our innovation labs, funding opportunities, industry conferences, and a network of 40,000+ professionals. You'll receive priority support for project proposals, access to proprietary research, and opportunities to collaborate on groundbreaking initiatives.
